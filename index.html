What are non-fungible tokens?

Non-fungible tokens, or NFTs, are digital assets that are unique and cannot be exchanged for other assets of the same type. In other words, you can't trade one Bitcoin for another Bitcoin, but you could trade one Bitcoin for an Ethereum. Similarly, you can't trade one NFT for another NFT, but you could trade an Ethereum for an NFT.

How to use opensea

  1. Visit a currency exchange to purchase the ETH you’ll need to pay for your transactions.
  2. Select the “Create” button on OpenSea.io.
  3. Select a wallet from among those listed, and follow the prompts to install it.
  4. Add ETH to your crypto wallet.
  5. Find the NFT you want to buy and click on its thumbnail image or name. You can also search for it using keywords or filtering by categories like rarity, timestamp and owner.
  6. Verify the purchase details before clicking “Buy Now” or “Bid.”
  7. After submitting your request, you will be prompted with a form where you enter your payment details. Pay close attention to the NFT token address and the amount of ETH you will be charged.
  8. Verify the transaction by entering your private key or seed phrase and click “Submit.” As long as the transaction is still pending on the network, you can cancel it and restart it later by clicking “Cancel Transaction.”
  9. OpenSea will show your purchased NFTs on your dashboard.

If you get stuck at any point, head to the Help Center to search for the information you need or click the “Submit a request” button.
